A documentary about Photography

This is a very cool documentary about photography about Ansel Adams done by Ric Burns. This documentary includes many things about his Ansels life and just different things about the photography that he used. This documentary has images, video, and readings from Ansel's own work. In order to get a more relistic feel for the subject matter that they were creating this documentary about, Burns and his coworkers retraced Adams steps. They climbed up rocks and hiked winding trails just so that they could make the documentary as believable and real as possible.
